The Problem Birame Solves

Attendees navigate venues, schedules and transport with no single tool
Paper tickets and accreditation cards are fragile and easily lost
Cash at event zones is slow - every stall needs a separate queue
Language barriers affect participants from 88 different Francophone nations

Load your JOJ Card with real money (XOF) using any of the payment methods on the left. Every 100 XOF you top up automatically earns 1 JOJ Point - credited instantly to your balance as you recharge, no extra steps.

Account Security Rules

Email normalized (lowercased + trimmed) before storage
Duplicate email rejected at registration
Password minimum 8 characters enforced
Session restored automatically on app launch
AuthGuard blocks every tab route if not logged in

Notification Feed - 6 Types

LIVE  ·  Real-time score alerts
Ticket  ·  Event countdown reminders
Shuttle  ·  Departure & seat availability
Goal  ·  Scorer + minute of goal
Wallet  ·  Top-up confirmed
Medal  ·  Gold / silver / bronze won

Unread notifications show a red badge on the bell icon in the home header. Tapping opens a slide-up sheet.

Theme Switcher

Dark Mode
Deep Navy · Glass surfaces
Light Mode
Clean white · Soft shadows

Toggled in the Profile screen. Preference saved to AsyncStorage and restored on next launch. Every screen reads getColors(state.theme) so the full app repaints instantly.
